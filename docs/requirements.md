# Requirements

## Functional

- Highly visible at night.
- Activity-based color and brightness.
- Smooth animations at low brightness.
- Battery-powered with safe charging.
- Weather-resistant and comfortable.

## Non-Functional

- Minimum 5 hours runtime at typical brightness.
- Single 21700 cell form factor.
- Safe thermal envelope for skin contact.
- Modular for GPS/BLE expansion.
